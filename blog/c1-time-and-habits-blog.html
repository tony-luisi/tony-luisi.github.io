Time and habits

My EDA experience

Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I tried the following techniques:

  • The power of meditation
  • How Mindfulness can improve productivity, focus, and work life balance
  • Pomodoro technique
  • Create a habit
  • Tiny habits / Fogg method

What did you find interesting?

The technique I found the most intersting was the Pomodoro technique. Splitting my time into 25 minute increments seems like an easy way to focus on specific tasks that I want to get completed. Furthermore, the technique is also about estimating how long is takes to perform each task I perform.

I also found the material around Tiny Habits and the Fogg method interesting. By doing big tasks incrementally at a time and not focusing on trying to accomplish the big picture all at once is a good way to make sure I can form long last repeatable habits.

What worked for you?

The techniques listed above I think are the ones that will work for me the most. The other techniques (e.g. growth mindset, counter-intuitive productivity, flow, etc) don't rreally appeal to me as either I am already aware and have used these techniques, or seem too much of an abstract concept to want to execute (I like practical solutions!).

Which will you experiment further with?

Going forward, I think I will focus on experimenting on meditation, the Pomodoro technique, and tiny habits.
