The Meditation Process

Week 3

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

Following the 'process over product' allowed me to manage my time by time-boxing each part of the process. It allowed me to get to the end goal without getting (too) distracted. I also learnt a lot about web design in the process.

What did you think about mediation before reading chapter 2?

I didn't know a lot about meditation before reading this chapter. Basically I thought it was just a way to be calm and relaxed (and you might occasionally meditate in Yoga).

What new things have you learnt about mediation?

That it is much more than being calm and relaxing. It's about being aware of yourself and your breathing, being aware that you exist (meta-attention). You don't have to take the usual meditation stance, as long as you are able to be alert and relaxed (although it is recommended in the advanced level). Meditation is a process, like exercise, that requires repetition and practice. The temporal technique appeals to me (where you close your eyes until you're sleeping, and open them until you're distracted).
